## Yclas side

1.  Log in to your  **admin panel**.
2.  Go to  **Configure**  >  **Integrations**  >  **Social** -> **Facebook**
3.  Enter the  **Facebook App ID**  into the  **Facebook comments**  box.
4.  Press  **Save**!
